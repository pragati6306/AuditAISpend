# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Problem

Engineering leaders at startups overspend on AI tools by 20-40%, often paying for overlapping features or scaling plans they've outgrown.

**Example:** ChatGPT Enterprise ($60/seat × 150 = $9,000/mo) when only 5 people use it intensively.

## Solution

**Credex Auditor** — Free, 2-minute audit that shows exactly where money is being wasted and suggests optimizations.

## Value Proposition

| Problem                                | Solution                               | Outcome                     |
| -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| "Is ChatGPT Team worth it for us?"     | Audit compares plans + team size       | Save $200-$500/month        |
| "Should we switch to Copilot?"         | Rule engine analyzes use case          | Switch recommendation + ROI |
| "How much are we really spending?"     | Dashboard shows current vs optimized   | Immediate visibility        |
| "Can we scale without cost explosion?" | Recommendations scale with team growth | Predictable spend           |

## Target Users

**Primary:** CTOs / VP Engineering at 5-30 person startups
**Secondary:** Finance ops, founders managing burn
**Segment:** B2B SaaS, AI-first companies, agencies

## Positioning

> **Credex Auditor** is the audit tool for AI tool spending. Like a financial advisor for Claude/ChatGPT budgets.

**Tagline:** "Save $300-$500/month on AI tools. Free audit, real savings."

## Distribution Channels

1. **Content Marketing**
   - "2024 AI Tool Benchmarks" — cost comparison guide
   - Post to HN, r/startups, founder communities
   - LinkedIn posts about AI cost optimization

2. **Product Hunt**
   - Launch as free tool for maximum reach
   - "Compare your AI spend to 100+ other startups" angle

3. **Partnerships**
   - Target founder communities (Y Combinator, SaaS Academy)
   - Partner with tech news outlets

4. **SEO**
   - Rank for "ChatGPT cost calculator," "Claude vs GPT pricing"
   - How-to guides on reducing AI spend

## Monetization (Phase 2)

- **Free tier** — Basic audit
- **Pro** ($5/mo) — Historical tracking, team comparison benchmarks
- **Enterprise** — Bulk discounts, integration with billing systems

## Success Metrics

- 1,000+ audits in first 30 days
- 20%+ of users implement top recommendation
- $X in affiliate/referral revenue from tool discounts
- Feature on 3+ tech media outlets

## Launch Timeline

**Day 1-3:** Finalize product  
**Day 4-5:** Prepare launch assets (screenshots, copy, landing page)  
**Day 6:** Soft launch to founder friends for feedback  
**Day 7:** Public launch (HN, Product Hunt, Twitter)
